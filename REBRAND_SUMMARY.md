# Soul Lab Gym Website Rebrand - Light Pastel Theme

## ✅ Project Complete

Successfully rebranded the Soul Lab Gym website from dark theme to a light, airy pastel aesthetic.

## Color Palette Applied

### Primary Colors
- **Soft Pink**: `#FFB6D9` (Tailwind: `pink-500`, `pink-100` variants)
- **Pastel Mint/Green**: `#A8E6D5` (Tailwind: `emerald-50`, `green-400` variants)
- **White & Grays**: Pure white backgrounds with gray text

### Background Colors
- **Primary**: White (`bg-white`)
- **Accents**: 
  - `bg-pink-50` - light pink tint
  - `bg-emerald-50` / `bg-green-50` - light green tint
  - `bg-gradient-to-b from-pink-50 to-white` - soft gradients
  - `bg-gradient-to-br` - diagonal blends

### Text Colors
- **Headings**: `text-gray-900` (dark on light)
- **Body**: `text-gray-700` and `text-gray-600`
- **Accents**: `text-pink-600`, `text-emerald-600`, `text-green-600`

### Button Styles
- **Primary CTAs**: `bg-gradient-to-r from-pink-500 to-green-400 hover:from-pink-600 hover:to-green-500`
- **Secondary**: Border buttons with gray-900 text on light backgrounds
- **Shadows**: Soft shadows with pink/green tints
